# TIPS FOR VEO 3.1

1. **Be Specific:** Include details about colors, style, mood
2. **Mention Animation:** Explicitly say "animated" or "animation"
3. **Indian Context:** Include "Indian" for culturally relevant results
4. **Color Theme:** Always mention "purple/violet" for brand consistency
5. **Style Consistency:** Use "modern 2D animation" or "flat illustration" throughout
6. **Camera Movement:** Add "camera zooms in" or "slow pan" for dynamism
